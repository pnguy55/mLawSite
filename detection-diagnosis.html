THE PROBLEM OF LATENT SYMPTOMS ∙ SEEK HELP IMMEDIATELY

One of the biggest problems of mesothelioma and asbestosis cases is that a long time can pass between the initial exposure to asbestos and the symptoms of mesothelioma surfacing. Sometimes decades can pass before a mesothelioma victim sees the onset of the illness. Early detection and diagnosis of mesothelioma is critical to making sure you get the medical care you need and secure your opportunity for the full and fair personal injury damages or workers’ compensation that you deserve.

Getting us on the case early allows us the opportunity to gather and preserve evidence, including video depositions. If the mesothelioma is life-threatening, we want to get this evidence early, and it also helps us to show how mesothelioma progressed through your system. Having this evidence in place early increases the power of your case.

If you have ever worked environment in which you were exposed to asbestos for any length of time, or if you are experiencing any symptoms of mesothelioma, it is important to:

Seek Medical Attention:

We can help you find a physician who specializes in mesothelioma and asbestosis cases who can give you an accurate diagnosis.

We offer free initial consultations, so you can talk with one of our experienced lawyers about your case.
