LET US PROTECT YOUR RIGHTS

Our personal injury|mesothelioma|malpractice|worker's comp|auto accident attorneys focus exclusively on helping injured people and their families. Our practice areas include:

Car Accidents

It is crucial to work with an experienced Baton Rouge car accident lawyer after any crash involving negligence on the part of another driver or a third party.

Dangerous Drugs

By pursuing class action or mass tort litigation against the manufacturer or a defective drug, groups of people who have been similarly harmed can obtain the benefits they deserve.

Truck Accidents

Nobody should suffer in silence after a collision with a big rig. Stand up for your rights with help from a personal injury lawyer.

Medical Malpractice

Doctors, surgeons and other medical professionals have the responsibility to treat their patients according to a reasonable standard of care in the medical community.

Mesothelioma & Asbestos

If you have mesothelioma, or if a loved one has passed away from cancer, there is a high likelihood that you’re eligible for financial recovery.

Defective Products

If you or a loved one has been hurt by a dangerous product or defective device, don’t let a deep-pocketed corporation victimize you again by refusing to pay the compensation you deserve.

Pedestrian Accidents

We will work with your doctor or other medical specialists to determine the medical care and rehabilitation services you will need to recover to the fullest extent possible.

Maritime Accidents

We provide aggressive representation in unseaworthiness claims and other cases where a vessel’s owners are liable for tort claim damages.

Workers' Compensation

We work tirelessly to help injured employees get the medical care and workers’ compensation benefits they deserve.

Boating Accidents

After a boating accident that leaves you with personal injuries, property damage, or other losses, consult with a personal injury attorney.

Premises Liability

Property owners in the state of Louisiana are responsible for keeping their property reasonably safe for other people.

Personal Injury

When you’ve been injured by someone else’s negligence, you need an attorney on your side who knows how to fight for your rights and who isn’t afraid to stand up for you.

Offshore Injuries

Our attorneys know maritime law, and we know the challenges injured maritime workers face as they deal with recovery, medical bills, lost income, and tough questions.

Class Action & Mass Tort

Class action and mass tort lawsuits offer an opportunity for groups of people, all of whom have been similarly wronged, to sue collectively.

Construction Accidents

As a construction worker, your ability to earn a living depends on your physical well-being. You owe it to yourself and your family to get proper compensation after an accident.

Social Security Disability

The disability claims process is difficult, and our experienced attorneys are here to help with the process.

Motorcyle Accidents

As a motorcyclist, you put yourself at risk every time you get behind the handlebars in Louisiana. If you're hurt, you shouldn't worry about finding good help.

Wrongful Death

When the death of a loved one is caused by the negligent or wrongful acts of another, you should have someone in your corner willing to work for compensation while you take time to grieve.
